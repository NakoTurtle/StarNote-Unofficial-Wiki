# 🌟 StarNote Unofficial Wiki

Welcome to the comprehensive unofficial feature guide for **StarNote**, a high-performance note-taking application designed for students and professionals. This wiki reflects features as of **v1.3.4 (April 2026)**.

---

## 🖋️ Writing & Input Engine
* **Variable Pen Tools:**
    * **Fountain Pen:** Pressure-sensitive for calligraphy and expressive writing.
    * **Ballpoint:** Uniform line width, ideal for technical diagrams.
    * **Highlighter:** Transparent ink that layers without obscuring underlying text.
* **SoftPaint Brush (v1.2.0+):** A specialized brush for soft-edge shading and artistic sketching.
* **Canvas Options:**
    * **Infinite Canvas:** Limitless scrolling in all directions—ideal for brainstorming or large flowcharts.
    * **Fixed-Size Pages:** Standard templates including A4, Letter, and Legal.
* **Smart Shapes:** A "Hold-to-Snap" gesture that automatically converts rough strokes into perfect geometric shapes (circles, triangles, rectangles, etc.).
* **Precision Control:** Dedicated **Palm Rejection** and **Pen-Only Mode** to prevent accidental touches from interfering with your stylus work.

---

## 📊 Organization & Data Management
* **Native Tables (v1.3.4):** Insert structured tables with adjustable rows and columns. Supports cell background coloring and direct handwriting within cells.
* **Advanced Layering:** Manage multiple transparent layers per page. Layers can be locked, hidden, or adjusted for opacity—perfect for complex engineering overlays.
* **Sticker & Element Library:** Save frequently used symbols, diagrams, or math snippets as "Stickers" for one-tap insertion.
* **Hierarchical Filing:** Organize notebooks into a system of **Nested Folders** (e.g., *Engineering > Year 3 > Electrotechnique*).
* **Outline View:** Generate a clickable Table of Contents within long documents for rapid navigation.

---

## 🎨 Visuals & Customization
* **Smart Inversion (Dark Mode):** A global dark mode that intelligently inverts paper and ink colors while preserving the original colors of inserted images and stickers.
* **Custom Paper Templates:** Import any PDF or PNG to serve as a recurring page background (useful for specialized engineering grids).
* **Dynamic UI:**
    * **Moveable Toolbar:** Dock the tool palette to the top, bottom, or sides.
    * **Floating Palette:** A compact, movable tool menu to maximize writing space.
* **Custom Color Palettes:** Supports HEX code input and a color dropper tool for precise aesthetic control.

---

## 📂 PDF & Document Handling
* **High-Fidelity Rendering:** Maintains sharpness at extreme zoom levels, essential for reading small-print data sheets or blueprints.
* **Active Annotations:** Robust engine for importing large PDF textbooks and adding annotations.
* **Hyperlink Support:** Full navigation for internal document links and external web URLs.
* **Side-by-Side View:** Open two notes, or a PDF and a note, simultaneously in a split-screen layout.
* **Flexible Exports:** Export as "Flattened PDFs" (permanent annotations) or "Editable PDFs" (layered objects).

---

## 🔐 Privacy, Sync & Security
* **Local-First Philosophy:** Primary data is stored on-device for maximum speed and offline reliability.
* **Google Drive Integration:** Semi-automatic backup and restore functionality to safeguard data across devices.
* **Biometric Security:** Secure specific notebooks or the entire app via fingerprint or passcode.
* **One-Time Purchase:** A lifetime license model that avoids recurring subscription fees.

---

## 🛠️ Roadmap & Community Requests
* **LaTeX Support:** Currently a top-requested feature for students to convert handwriting into digital formulas.
* **Handwriting Search (OCR):** While typed text is searchable, searching handwritten ink is in active development.
* **Real-Time Auto-Sync:** Developers are currently working on a seamless "Edit on Tablet, View on Phone" sync capability.

# 🛠️ Getting Started with StarNote
Welcome to the community! This guide will walk you through setting up StarNote for the first time, from installation to optimizing your workflow.
## 1. Installation
StarNote is currently available for mobile and tablet devices. Use the links below to ensure you are downloading the official version:

 * **iOS / iPadOS:** Download on the [App Store](https://apps.apple.com/us/app/starnote-handwriting-pdf/id6751570915) 
 * **Android:** Download on [Google Play](https://play.google.com/store/apps/details?id=com.onyx.galaxy.global.note) or use the direct apk download on their [website](https://site.starnote.ai/download).

## 2. Initial Configuration
When you first open the app, we recommend configuring these three core settings to ensure your data is safe and your experience is smooth.
### **Cloud Backup**
By default, notes are stored locally on your device. To access them across devices or ensure they are backed up:

 1. Go to **Settings** > **Cloud backup**.
 2. Select and bind your preferred provider (OneDrive or Google Drive).
 3. Toggle **Automatic backup** to ON.

!!! info "Importnant!"
    Cloud backup is different from live syncing, which is not yet implemented. Live syncing is still in development and is expected to launch late Q2.

### **Stylus & Pencil Optimization**
If you are using an Apple Pencil, S-Pen, or third-party stylus, calibrate it for the best handwriting experience:

 * **Pressure Sensitivity:** Adjust the pressure sensitivity in your pen settings to match your writing style.
 * **Palm Rejection:** Ensure "Palm Rejection" is enabled in gesture settings if you write with your hand resting on the screen.

### **Default Templates**
Save time by setting your favorite paper style as the default for all new quick notes:

 * Navigate to **Settings** > **Document settings** > **Quick note default template**.
 * Choose from the built in or custom templates.

## 3. Core Features Overview
To get the most out of the app's infinite canvas, keep these basic gestures in mind:

| Action | Gesture |
|---|---|
| **Pan / Scroll** | Drag with two fingers |
| **Zoom In/Out** | Pinch with two fingers |
| **Erase** | Use the eraser tool |
| **Select / Lasso** | Draw a circle around elements using the lasso tool to move or resize them |

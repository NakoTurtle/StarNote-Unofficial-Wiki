# 🖋️ Pens & Writing Tools

StarNote’s proprietary vector ink engine ensures that every stroke remains crisp at any zoom level. Unlike raster-based apps, your text and diagrams will never pixelate, regardless of how far you zoom into the infinite canvas.

---

## 🖌️ The Five Core Pens
Select a pen type to see its specific characteristics and best-use cases for your studies.

=== "Fountain Pen"
    * **Best For:** Expressive headings and personal signatures.
    * **Behavior:** Highly responsive to pressure(adjustable); mimics the flow of a real nib.
    * **Note:** Width is determined solely by downward pressure.

=== "Calligraphy Pen"
    * **Best For:** Titles and stylized labels.
    * **Behavior:** Uses a "chiseled" nib logic where line thickness changes based on the **direction** of your stroke.
    * **Note:** Excellent for making module covers look professional.

=== "Softbrush Pen"
    * **Best For:** General sketching and bold emphasis.
    * **Behavior:** A softer, more organic edge compared to the technical pens. 
    * **Note:** Provides a consistent "inky" feel.

=== "Ballpoint Pen"
    * **Best For:** Technical derivations and active note-taking.
    * **Behavior:** The most stable and predictable pen. It maintains a consistent width regardless of speed or pressure.

=== "Pencil"
    * **Best For:** Drafting, brainstorming, and light annotations.
    * **Behavior:** Mimics graphite texture. 
    * **Note:** Perfect for initial layouts before "inking" over them with the Ballpoint or Fountain pen.

## ⚙️ Writing Settings & Optimization

<div class="grid cards" markdown>

-   :material-gesture-tap-hold:{ .lg .middle } __Shape Recognition__
    ---
    Draw any basic shape and **hold** at the end of the stroke. StarNote will snap the line into a perfect geometric vector.

-   :material-some-stroke:{ .lg .middle } __Pressure Sensitivity__
    ---
    Adjust the sensitivity curve in **Settings > Stylus**. (Applicable to Fountain Pen and Pencil).

-   :material-hand-back-left:{ .lg .middle } __Palm Rejection__
    ---
    Toggle **Stylus Only Mode** in the top toolbar to prevent accidental "ghost strokes" from your hand.

-   :material-vector-curve:{ .lg .middle } __Stroke Stabilization__
    ---
    The engine automatically smooths handwriting jitters to keep your notes looking clean at high zoom levels.
